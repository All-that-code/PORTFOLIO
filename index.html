ABOUT

Zubizu Studio is the storefront for Amaia Arbizu, a bilingual multimedia designer, illustrator and art director based in northern Spain.

With extensive international experience as an in-house and freelance designer, her skill set combines varied media technical knowledge with a ‘big-picture’ approach to design, creativity, and great people skills acquired during a past life as a well-being business owner.

She's passionate about acquiring new skills and knowledge and is currently involved in AI-related training. In her free time, she loves staying active through strength training and yoga.
